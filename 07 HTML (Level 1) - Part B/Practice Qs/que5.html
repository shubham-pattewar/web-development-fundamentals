Hr tag in HTML

The hr HTML element represents a thematic break between paragraph-level elements: for example, a change of scene in a story, or a shift of topic within a section.

HTML Entities

HTML entities are special codes used to represent characters that are reserved in HTML or characters that may not appear on a standard keyboard. They ensure that these characters are displayed correctly in a web browser.

  • Copyright Symbol: ©
  • Registered Trademark: ®
  • Square Root: √
  • Summation: ∑
  • Infinity: ∞
  • Dollar: $
  • Yen: ¥
